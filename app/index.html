Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add this app to my Home Screen?

iPhone/iPad: Tap the 'Share' button in Safari, then scroll down and tap 'Add to Home Screen'.

Android: Tap the three-dot menu in Chrome, then tap 'Install app' or 'Add to Home screen'.

How does the plan work?

The app calculates the number of verses you need to memorize each day to reach your target date. The "Today's Verses" section shows verses you've already completed (in gray) plus the new verses for the current day (highlighted).

How do I mark progress?

Click the "Mark Today Complete" button at the bottom of the "Today's Verses" section. This will move your progress forward and highlight the next set of verses for the following day.

How do I share a plan?

Open the menu and click "Share Plan." This will copy a special link to your clipboard. When someone opens this link, the passage you are memorizing will be pre-selected for them, and they just need to choose their own target date.

Can I select a part of a chapter?

Yes. If you select a passage that is a full chapter (like "Romans 8"), dropdowns will appear allowing you to select a specific start and end verse for your plan.

How do I reset my plan?

Open the menu and click "Reset Plan." This will completely clear your current progress and passage selection, allowing you to start a new plan from scratch.
